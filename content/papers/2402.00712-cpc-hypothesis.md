abstract_en: |
  Understanding the emergence of symbol systems, especially language, requires
  a computational model that reproduces both the developmental learning process
  in everyday life and the evolutionary dynamics of symbol emergence throughout
  history. This paper proposes the Collective Predictive Coding (CPC) hypothesis,
  framing symbol emergence as decentralized Bayesian inference performed jointly
  by interacting agents.
