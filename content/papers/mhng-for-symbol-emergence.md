abstract_en: |
  We propose the Metropolis-Hastings Naming Game (MHNG), a probabilistic
  generative model that derives the dynamics of symbol emergence as Bayesian
  inference. Acceptance and rejection of partner messages are formalised as
  Metropolis-Hastings steps, providing a principled bridge between multi-agent
  communication and posterior sampling.
