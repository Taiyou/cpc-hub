abstract_en: |
  This article presents active inference as a unifying process theory for
  perception, action, and learning under the free-energy principle. Behaviour
  is cast as inference over policies that minimise expected free energy,
  combining epistemic (information-seeking) and pragmatic (goal-directed)
  drives in a single objective.
